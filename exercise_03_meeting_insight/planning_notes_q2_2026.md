## Q2 priorities — ranked

### Priority 1 · Accelerate PLG conversion funnel
The self-serve channel is our most efficient — CAC of $210 vs $1,480 outbound. The team agreed to invest heavily here in Q2.

**Key initiatives:**
- In-app onboarding redesign (reduce time-to-value from 11 days to <5)
- Trial-to-paid nudge sequence (currently no automated touchpoints)
- Usage-based expansion triggers (accounts >80% of seat limit)

**Owner:** Priya (Product) + Growth squad
**Target:** Increase PLG conversion rate from 8.0% to 11%

---

### Priority 2 · Enterprise retention programme
Q1 churn was concentrated in the 50–200 seat enterprise tier. Root cause: delayed onboarding and lack of dedicated support during the Nova Dashboard rollout.

**Key initiatives:**
- Assign CSM to all accounts >$1,500 MRR (currently only >$3K)
- Launch quarterly business review (QBR) programme — first QBRs in May
- Build health score dashboard for CS team (data analyst hire required)

**Owner:** Customer Success (Marcus Webb interim until CSM hired)
**Target:** Enterprise NRR from 108% → 120% by end of Q2

---

### Priority 3 · Data & reporting foundation
Multiple teams are blocked on insights because the data stack is inconsistent. This was called out as a recurring friction point across product, CS, and growth.

**Key initiatives:**
- Hire data analyst (JD live, targeting May 1 start)
- Consolidate event tracking across web and product (currently 3 schemas)
- Deliver self-serve reporting dashboard for CS and sales by June 15

**Owner:** Daniel Osei (Engineering) + future data analyst hire
**Target:** Single source of truth for key metrics by June 30
